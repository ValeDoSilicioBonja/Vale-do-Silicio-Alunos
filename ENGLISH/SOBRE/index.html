Silicon Valley is in the Periphery

Transforming the Future of the Periphery with Technology. "Silicon Valley is in the Periphery" is an innovative project that aims to bring information technology (IT) education to young people in peripheral areas, offering a training pathway to a rapidly growing digital market. Through this IT course, the project seeks to provide participants with quality training, preparing them to work in various areas of the tech sector, which is one of the most promising and in-demand industries in the job market.

The Marli Medeiros Cultural Center (CEMME) has been operating for over 15 years, promoting social activities for children and adolescents in the Bom Jesus neighborhood, with the aim of improving the community and guaranteeing social rights.

Francyne Taboada

Francyne Taboada da Rosa, 34 years old, is a psychologist who graduated in 2013/2, with experience in social projects and working with children and adolescents in situations of social vulnerability. Her career includes working in several social assistance programs, such as the Todos Somos Porto Alegre program, Educational Work program, Family Assistance Service (SAF), and the Child Support and Strengthening Service. At the Marli Medeiros Cultural Center (CEMME), she began in 2011 as a Social Educator in the Projovem Leste program until 2013. Later, she returned to CEMME as the coordinator of the Projovem Leste program, a position she has held since 2019. In 2024, Francyne was elected as the Municipal Councilor for Children and Adolescents (CMDCA) of Porto Alegre, representing the OSC CEMME, where she continues to contribute to public policies aimed at the protection of children and adolescents.
